TrendyPK Opens 3 New Stores in Karachi, Targets Young Women's Casual Segment

By Reuters Pakistan | April 22, 2026

In a major retail expansion, TrendyPK has announced the opening of 3 new stores in the bustling region of Karachi. This move, planned since 2026-04, marks a significant step in the brand's aggressive growth strategy.

Targeting the lucrative "women's casual wear" segment, particularly ages 22 to 32, TrendyPK is rolling out a pricing model that boasts an estimated 30% pricing advantage over its major rivals.
